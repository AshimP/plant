Plant Care Guide
Simple Care

Keep your plants healthy without overthinking it

Good plant care is mostly about the basics done properly. Right light, sensible watering, and decent drainage solve more problems than panic.

  • Water only when the top soil feels dry
  • Keep bright-light plants near filtered sunlight
  • Always use pots with drainage holes
  • Ask on WhatsApp if you are unsure about placement
